Welcome to CoursiQuity!

Because making a schedule should be painless!

What is CoursiQuity?

As a college student, you have the option to take hundreds of General Education courses to experiment with a program of study, and that's great! Until you have too many options. You end up taking too much time to create a schedule, and you may feel overwhelmed with the options.

You may take a class that you regret or not choose the right courses to be able to graduate on time, and everyone wants to graduate on time!

This is where CoursiQuity comes in! We help you stay on track with your General Education Requirements, so all you have to worry about is taking courses that you enjoy (don't worry, we'll soon create functionality to pick classes for you based on your interests)!

How to Use CoursiQuity

Sign Up

Sign up by pressing the "Sign Up Instead" Button at the bottom of the screen.

After, you will receive an email asking you to verify your email by pressing the link sent in the email.

Sign In

After you verify your email, press the "Sign In Instead Button" on the screen and then press the orange "Sign In" button.

Choose Previous Courses

After you sign in, you will be brought to a screen where you can select courses that you have taken in the class.

First select the major, then the course within that major, and finally select the "Add" button to add the course to your list. When you are done, press the orange "Move On" button in the top right of the screen.

Choose Courses For the Future

After you select courses that you previously took, you will be directed to a screen where you will select courses that you plan on taking. When you are done, press the "Finish Up" button on the top right of the screen.

Perform Search

When you select courses that you will take in the future, you will be brought to the screen to do a search. Select on courses that you want to take, and the list will get smaller as you select courses. When you selected all of the courses, the app will tell you that you have selected courses that have fulfilled all of your outcomes.

View Profile

After your search, you can go back to your Profile Screen. The courses on the list on the right are courses that you can take that will fulfill all of your General Education outcomes.
